Staying long

If you stay in the Netherlands for 4 months or longer, you must register as a resident with the municipality where you live. You must do so within 5 days after your arrival in the Netherlands. If your partner and/or children also stay in the Netherlands for 4 months or longer, they must also register with the municipality.
